The Platform

Seven tools.
One subscription.

Everything a food truck operator actually needs — built by someone who understands the hustle, not a VC looking for a cut of every booking.

01
📋
Request Marketplace

Verified event planners post catering jobs with dates, budgets, and guest counts. You submit interest privately. No comment pile-ons. No shouting into the void.

Core feature
02
📅
Event Calendar

Browse real events up to two years out — festivals, community gatherings, farmers markets, HOA events. Never miss a big one because it got buried in a feed.

Public access
03
🅿️
Parking & Real Estate

Property owners list available spots — daily, weekly, or permanent. Storage or active selling locations. Trucks post what they need. Simple match, no middleman.

Free to list
04
🏢
Venue Partnerships

Bars, breweries, coffee shops, and retail locations post recurring truck partnership slots. You find the steady Tuesday night spot without cold-calling anyone.

Free for venues
05
🛠️
Vendor Services

Verified insurance agents, equipment repair, license specialists, POS support, and supplies — all in one place. Stop hunting through Facebook comments for a hood repair referral.

Vetted providers
06
💼
Jobs Board

Post open positions — cooks, window operators, drivers, catering staff, managers. Job seekers browse and apply for free. Positions are tied to verified truck profiles so applicants know exactly who they're working for.

Free for job seekers
07
🔒
Verified Deposit Protection

When an organizer collects vendor fees through ServiceWindow, funds are held in escrow until both parties confirm the event terms were honored. If an organizer changes terms after payment — like cutting a two-day event to one day and keeping your full fee — you have recourse. No more $550 lessons learned the hard way.

Trust & Safety

No scammers.
Structurally impossible.

Facebook lets anyone post anything. ServiceWindow doesn't. Verification isn't a checkbox — it's the foundation every single interaction is built on.

🪪
Business License Verification
All food trucks submit their FL DBPR or business license number, verified before their profile goes live. No license, no listing.
📱
Dual-Channel Account Confirmation
Every account confirms via phone and email before posting anything. No burner accounts. No anonymous requests allowed.
⭐
Two-Way Reviews After Every Job
Trucks rate requesters. Requesters rate trucks. Bad actors are flagged and removed. Your reputation is your most valuable asset — we protect it.
🔒
Contact Info Hidden Until Matched
No phone numbers or emails exposed publicly. Pedro shouldn't have to post his cell to 34,000 strangers to get a catering gig. Now he doesn't.
💰
Deposit Escrow Protection
Vendor fees held until event terms are confirmed by both parties. Organizers can't change the rules after you've paid. Your $550 is protected.
