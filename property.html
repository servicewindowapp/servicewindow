Listing Types

What truck operators are looking for.

From overnight parking to permanent commissary space — if you have it, operators need it.

🅿️

Daily & Weekend Parking

High-traffic lots, commercial areas, shopping centers. Trucks want spots where customers already come — your lot becomes their location.

🌙

Overnight & Weekly Storage

Secure lots for overnight truck storage. Most operators run out of residential neighborhoods — HOA rules create constant demand for off-site storage.

🏭

Commissary Kitchen Access

Licensed commercial kitchens for prep work. Required by most county health codes for food truck operations — serious demand, limited supply.

🏢

Commercial Lots & Pads

Dedicated truck pads, permanent spots with utility hookups. Long-term leases to operators who want a reliable home base.

🔧

Maintenance & Service Space

Covered bays, wash stations, or mechanical access. Trucks need more than a parking spot — service space has a premium in SWFL.

📦

Dry Storage & Warehouse

Storage units, warehouse bays, or climate-controlled space for supplies, equipment, and inventory overflow.
