# How to Add Your ServiceWindow Link — Instructions for Truck Operators

Sharing your ServiceWindow profile link helps new customers find and book you. Here's how to add it in under 5 minutes.

---

## Step 1 — Find Your Profile Link

1. Log into your truck dashboard at **servicewindow.app**
2. Click **View My Profile** (top of the dashboard)
3. Copy the URL from your browser — it will look like:
   `https://servicewindow.app/truck-profile.html?id=XXXXXXXX`

That is your booking link. Use it everywhere below.

---

## Step 2 — Add It to Facebook

**Facebook Page Bio (About section):**

1. Go to your Facebook Page
2. Click **Edit Page Info** (or **Edit** on your About section)
3. Find the **Website** field
4. Paste your ServiceWindow link
5. Save

**Facebook Page Bio (short description):**

Add this line to your Page's short description (under your page name):

> Book us on ServiceWindow → [paste your link]

---

## Step 3 — Add It to Instagram

1. Go to your Instagram profile
2. Tap **Edit Profile**
3. In the **Website** field, paste your ServiceWindow link
4. In the **Bio** field, add this line:

> Book us at servicewindow.app

5. Tap **Done**

---

## Step 4 — Add It to Your Google Business Profile (optional but recommended)

If your food truck has a Google Business listing:

1. Go to **business.google.com**
2. Select your listing
3. Click **Edit profile** → **Business information**
4. Find the **Website** field
5. Paste your ServiceWindow link
6. Save

This lets customers who find you on Google Maps book you directly.

---

## Suggested Post (optional)

Once your link is added, post this to your Facebook page and Instagram:

> We're now on ServiceWindow — Southwest Florida's verified food truck marketplace. You can browse our menu, check availability, and send us a booking request directly through our profile. Link in bio.
