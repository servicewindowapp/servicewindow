Before You Book a Food Truck
Food trucks are mobile restaurants. Most require a minimum of 75–150 guests to make an appearance worthwhile.
Expect a minimum spend guarantee — typically $500–$1,500 depending on the truck and event type.
Travel radius matters. Trucks in SWFL generally serve within 30–60 miles of their home base.
For private parties under 50 people, consider whether a food truck is the right fit — catering may be more cost-effective.
55+ communities, HOA events, and corporate gatherings are ideal — trucks love recurring, high-volume stops.
