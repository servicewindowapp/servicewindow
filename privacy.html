Privacy Policy

Effective: April 1, 2026  ·  ServiceWindow LLC

1. Who We Are

ServiceWindow ("we," "our," or "us") operates servicewindow.app, a verified food truck marketplace for Southwest Florida. We connect food truck operators with event organizers, HOA managers, venues, and property owners across Lee, Collier, Sarasota, Charlotte, and Hendry counties.

2. Information We Collect

Account information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, phone number, business name, and role on the platform (truck operator, organizer, venue, etc.).

Profile information: Business details you voluntarily provide — cuisine type, service radius, certifications, biography, website, and links to third-party delivery platforms.

Booking and messaging data: Records of booking requests, acceptances, and messages exchanged between users on the platform.

Payment information: We do not store credit card numbers. Payments are processed by Stripe. We store subscription status and Stripe customer identifiers only.

Usage data: Standard server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited. We do not use tracking pixels or third-party ad networks.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the ServiceWindow platform
  • To verify user identity and business credentials
  • To facilitate bookings and communications between users
  • To send transactional emails (booking confirmations, account notices) via Resend
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe
  • To comply with applicable law

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties except as described in Section 4.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We share data with the following service providers solely to operate the platform:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage
  • Stripe — subscription billing and payment processing
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Cloudflare — DNS and network security
  • GitHub Pages — static site hosting

Each provider has their own privacy policy governing their use of data. We do not authorize these providers to use your data for their own purposes.

5. Public Profile Information

If you are a verified truck operator, your profile — including business name, cuisine type, service area, and bio — is visible to other users on the marketplace. You control what information you add to your profile. Do not include sensitive personal information in public-facing profile fields.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you close your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or legitimate business necessity (e.g., billing records).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Contact us to make a CCPA request.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS), row-level security on our database, and limited access controls. No system is perfectly secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.

9. Children's Privacy

ServiceWindow is intended for business users and adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the platform evolves. We will notify active users by email of material changes. Continued use of the platform after notice constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
