The Platform

Everything a food truck
actually needs.

01 📋
Request Marketplace

Verified requesters post events, catering jobs, and pop-up opportunities with real budgets attached. Trucks submit interest privately — no 100-person comment wars. You get the lead, not a lottery ticket.

02 📅
Event Calendar

Browse and book events up to two years out. Big festivals, farmers markets, corporate gigs, and 55+ community events all in one place. Filter by date, event type, and guest count — never miss a big one again.

03 🅿️
Parking & Real Estate

Property owners list available spots — permanent pads, event-day locations, or overnight storage. Food trucks post what they need. Private lots, commercial spaces, event venues: all in one board.

04 🛡️
Verified Profiles Only

Every account — truck, requester, or property owner — is verified before posting. Business license checks, phone confirmation, and a two-way rating system keep scammers out and build trust on both sides of every transaction.

Trust & Safety

No scammers.
Guaranteed.

Facebook lets anyone post anything. RollCall doesn't. Every account is verified before a single message is sent — protecting both sides of every booking.

🪪
Business License Verification
All food trucks submit their FL DBPR or business license number, verified before their profile goes live.
📱
Phone + Email Confirmation
Every account — requester, truck, or property owner — confirms via dual-channel verification. No burner accounts.
⭐
Two-Way Ratings After Every Job
Trucks rate requesters. Requesters rate trucks. Bad actors are flagged and removed. Your reputation travels with you.
🚫
No Public Contact Info Until Matched
Phone numbers and emails stay hidden until both parties accept a connection. No spam. No stalkers. No harassment.
