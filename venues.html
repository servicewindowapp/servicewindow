How it works for venues
Free to join. No commission on residencies. Direct communication with trucks.
1
List your space
Create a free venue profile with your space details, capacity, amenities, and available dates. Verified trucks browse the Venue Partnership board and send you inquiries.
2
Browse & invite trucks
Search 83 verified trucks by cuisine, service type, and availability. Send a direct inquiry to any truck — they respond within 24 hours or we flag them as unresponsive.
3
Book direct, keep the revenue
Confirm details in the platform, agree on terms, and book. Revenue-share residencies cost you nothing. For private events, terms are set directly between you and the truck.
