Our Story

The problem was obvious. The solution wasn't.

In Southwest Florida, the food truck industry runs on Facebook groups. 34,900 members in one group alone — HOA managers posting booking requests and getting buried under 44 unorganized comments. Trucks shouting availability into a feed that never stops scrolling. And scammers. Constant scam warnings, posted by admins every single day.

The demand was real. The supply was real. The infrastructure connecting them was a social media platform that was never designed for it.

ServiceWindow was built to fix that. A purpose-built marketplace where every truck is verified, every booking request gets a structured response, and HOA managers can find the right truck for their 950-home community without posting in a comment thread.

We started with Region 1 — 21 cities across Lee, Collier, Sarasota, Charlotte, and Hendry counties — because SWFL has one of the most active food truck communities in Florida and the clearest gap between what the market needs and what currently exists.

What We Stand For

The platform is built around three things.

Everything else is secondary.

Verification First

Every truck on ServiceWindow is reviewed before going live. Permits, licenses, certifications — the platforms that skip this create the scam environment everyone's trying to escape.

Geo-Locked by Design

SWFL only. No posts from West Palm Beach polluting your feed. No trucks from Orlando showing up in Fort Myers search results. Local focus is a feature, not a limitation.

Free for the Demand Side

Event planners, HOA managers, and venues never pay. The demand side is the flywheel — keeping it free keeps it growing, which keeps trucks paying, which keeps the platform solvent.
