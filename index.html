The Problem We're Solving
Facebook groups are a scammer's playground

Two documented scam operations are actively targeting SWFL food truck operators right now.

🚨 Scam #1 — Event Post Scraping
Scrapers monitor the groups, copy legitimate event posts, create fake Google Forms, and collect upfront vendor fees. The "organizer" disappears after payment.
Real case: Truck paid $550 vendor fee. Event terms changed 6 months later. No refund.
🚨 Scam #2 — Piggyback Hijacking
Scammers monitor trucks responding to real event posts, then DM those trucks pretending to be the organizer — requesting $100–$200 "vendor fees."
Real case: Barb McGowan responded to a Pelican Lakes poker run post, got an email requesting a $100 fee. Community confirmed it was a scam.
🛡️

ServiceWindow eliminates both — by design

All transactions happen inside the platform between verified users. Vendor fees go into escrow — not a stranger's PayPal. Verified identity on both sides means scammers can't get an account.
